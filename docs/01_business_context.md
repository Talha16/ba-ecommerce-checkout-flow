# Business Context – E-Commerce Checkout Flow

## Company Overview
NovaCart is a fictional online retailer specializing in consumer electronics and accessories. Customers can browse products, add items to a cart, and complete purchases through a web-based storefront.

## Problem Statement
NovaCart has identified a high rate of cart abandonment during the checkout process. Customers frequently exit the flow due to confusing address entry, unclear error messages, and friction during payment.

## Business Objective
The objective of this initiative is to design a clearer, more reliable checkout experience that:
- Reduces cart abandonment
- Simplifies address and payment entry
- Provides clear feedback when errors occur
- Supports future analytics and optimization

## Scope of This Analysis
This analysis focuses on the checkout journey starting from:
- “Proceed to Checkout” on the cart page  
and ending with:
- Order confirmation after successful payment

Out of scope:
- Product browsing
- Account registration
- Returns and refunds
